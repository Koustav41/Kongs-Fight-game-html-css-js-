# 🎮 Kong's Fight Game

Welcome to **Kong's Fight**, an exciting browser-based fighting game built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript! Challenge yourself against intelligent AI opponents or face off against other players in thrilling combat.

---

## ✨ Features

🎯 **Multiple Game Modes**
- **Single Player**: Battle against computer-controlled gorillas with increasing difficulty
- **Two Player**: Local multiplayer combat on the same device
- **Auto Play**: Watch two AI opponents face off automatically

🌬️ **Dynamic Wind Mechanics**
- Realistic wind effects that influence projectile trajectory
- Visual windmill indicator showing wind direction and speed
- Strategic gameplay element requiring skill and adaptation

🎨 **Theme Support**
- Light mode for daytime gaming
- Dark mode for comfortable nighttime play
- Seamless theme switching

💣 **Realistic Physics**
- Gravity simulation
- Wind-affected trajectory
- Collision detection with buildings
- Blast hole creation from impacts

🏗️ **Destructible Environment**
- Procedurally generated cityscape
- Buildings that respond to bomb impacts
- Progressive destruction as the game continues

📊 **Real-time Statistics**
- Angle and velocity tracking
- Wind speed monitoring
- Player performance metrics displayed in-game

🖥️ **Responsive Design**
- Full-screen support
- Optimized for various screen sizes
- Smooth animations and rendering

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Start the Game**: Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Select Game Mode**: Choose between Single Player, Two Player, or Auto Play
3. **Aim and Throw**: 
   - Click and drag the bomb to aim
   - The trajectory line shows where your projectile will go
   - Watch the angle and velocity meters
4. **Factor in Wind**: Check the windmill and wind speed indicator
5. **Hit Your Opponent**: Land your shot on the enemy gorilla to win
6. **Switch Turns**: After each throw, turns alternate between players
7. **Victory**: First to hit their opponent wins the round!
